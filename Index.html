Autism helper


This app helps people who have Autism.

By using this app you can clear out the extra voices by recording someone else's sound and will translate that into a robotic voice similarly the one having Autism can also type what they want to speak and the app will speak it for them in a robotic voice.

Type and

Click the button to speak


Click the button to translate what the other person is saying

and

will come out in a robotic voice back to you.
